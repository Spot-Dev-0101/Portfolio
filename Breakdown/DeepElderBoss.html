Scene

For a still render like this the only thing that matters is what's in camera. So for this scene there is the subject close to the camera and the ground behind it gets smaller aswell as grass infront of the camera gives the illusion of a larger scene.

The ground under the gun is the same as the background, but shrunk down to make it look like very high detailed moss. Unreal's Nanite allows me to use MegaScan assets with millions of polys while still being performant.

To make the gun look like it's been laying there for a while there is a dirt decal covering the top. This stops it looking clean and adds even more detail.
